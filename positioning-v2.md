## 1. Who We're Talking To

This is not a CTO evaluating AI platforms. This is a Director of Property Management at a 15-property retail portfolio who spent yesterday chasing a tenant for an expired COI, took an after-hours HVAC call at 9pm, and just lost her operations coordinator for the third time in two years.

### The buyer profile

**Title:** Director of Property Management, VP of Operations, Asset Manager, Regional Property Manager

**Company:** Commercial or retail property operator. 3-50 properties. 20-500+ tenants. Likely uses Yardi or MRI as their system of record. May or may not have a BPO answering service.

**What their week actually looks like:**
- Monday: 14 tenant emails already waiting. Two maintenance requests from the weekend. A vendor showed up without a current COI and got turned away - tenant is furious about the delay.
- Tuesday: Chasing three tenants for expired certificates of insurance. Again. The spreadsheet says renewal was due last month. Nobody followed up.
- Wednesday: Missed a rent escalation notice that should have gone out 60 days ago. Now you've lost leverage and your asset manager is asking questions.
- Thursday: After-hours HVAC request. The answering service took a message but didn't know the building's after-hours fee policy. Tenant is upset. You're on the phone at 10pm.
- Friday: Your operations coordinator put in their notice. They're the only person who knows how the compliance tracking spreadsheet works.

**What they care about (in order):**
1. Not drowning. Getting through the week without something falling through the cracks.
2. Keeping tenants satisfied so they renew.
3. Staying compliant so ownership doesn't get exposed.
4. NOI. Reducing operating costs without cutting corners.
5. Going home at a reasonable hour.

**What they do NOT care about:**
- What AI model powers the system
- Whether it's "agentic" or "generative"
- Category definitions like "managed AI operations"
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Your architecture diagram

**How they talk about this problem:**
- "I spend half my day chasing people"
- "Every time someone leaves, I'm rebuilding from scratch"
- "The answering service is useless - they don't know anything about the building"
- "I know we're missing lease deadlines but I can't track everything manually"
- "I need help, not another tool I have to manage"

### The insight that shapes everything

> **They don't want a better tool. They want someone to take this off their plate.**

This is the single most important insight. The 55% of CRE professionals who say they're "interested in AI" but haven't adopted it (Building Engines/BOMA 2025) aren't waiting for better features. They're waiting for something they don't have to implement, staff, train, QA, and maintain on top of everything else they're already drowning in.

Premise AI isn't asking them to adopt a tool. It's telling them: hand us the work. We'll do it.

## 4. The Three Things We Take Over

*(Not "functions" or "modules" - things the buyer does today that they'll stop doing)*

### 1. "We answer your tenants and vendors."
Every email, text, and chat - maintenance requests, HVAC, building questions, access requests, vendor onboarding, follow-ups. AI handles 70-90% on the spot. Our team handles escalations, emergencies, and anything sensitive.

Structured request management is built in. Vendor access approvals, amenity bookings, key card requests, service requests, maintenance intake - tenants and vendors are guided through a structured process that captures the right information upfront instead of sending you a freeform email you have to decode.

**What changes for you:** Your team stops being the first responder. Tenants get answers in under 2 hours, every time, including after hours. Nobody falls through the cracks.

**The number:** < 2 hour response time, guaranteed. After hours included.

### 2. "We chase, collect, and verify your compliance."
Tenant COIs - chased at renewal, collected, read against your lease requirements, verified, archived. Retail sales data - collected monthly, verified against percentage rent clauses, certified. Vendor insurance and credentialing - managed from onboarding through renewal.

**What changes for you:** Your team stops spending 8-20 hours per tenant per year chasing paperwork. You open a dashboard and see green or red for every tenant. That's it.

**The number:** 95%+ tenant COI compliance rate, guaranteed. QuadReal went from 500 checks/year to 3,500 without adding staff.

### 3. "We send every lease notice on time."
Rent escalations, renewal windows, CAM estimates, percentage rent true-ups - triggered from your lease data at the right time, sent to the right tenant, with the right numbers. Every response captured and escalated.

**What changes for you:** You stop tracking deadlines in spreadsheets. You never miss a 60-day notice window again. When a tenant responds, it's routed to the right person automatically.

**The number:** 100% on-time delivery rate for lease event communications.

## 5. Why This Isn't Software and Isn't a BPO

*(This is the differentiation section - but written for someone who's been burned by both)*

### The problem with software:
You buy the tool. Then you have to hire someone to run it. Train them. QA the AI responses. Maintain the knowledge base. Handle every escalation. And when that person leaves (and they will - turnover in this industry runs 30-50%), you start over. You didn't buy a solution. You bought a project.

### The problem with your BPO:
They answer the phone. They take a message. They don't know your building's after-hours HVAC policy. They don't know what a CAM reconciliation is. They definitely can't read a lease. And they don't chase COIs, collect sales data, or send renewal notices. You're paying $1.50/minute for someone to say "I'll pass that along."

### What Premise actually is:
You pay a flat monthly fee. We set up in two weeks. We learn your buildings, your policies, your leases. Then we take over.

| | Your BPO | A software tool | Premise AI |
|-|----------|----------------|------------|
| Answers tenant contacts | Takes a message | You still answer them | AI resolves 70-90%. Our team handles the rest. |
| Chases expired COIs | No | Sends you a reminder to chase them | We chase, collect, verify, and archive them. |
| Collects retail sales data | No | No | We collect, verify, and certify it monthly. |
| Sends lease notices on time | No | Alerts you to send them | We send them. On time. Every one. |
| Knows your building policies | No | Only if you set it up and maintain it | We build and maintain the knowledge base. |
| Works when your coordinator quits | Same | Breaks | Nothing changes. |
