## 1. The Problem We're Solving (For Rafi's Buyer)

Commercial landlords run their tenant operations with a patchwork that breaks every six months.

A generic BPO answers the phone but doesn't know what a CAM reconciliation is. An internal coordinator chases COIs manually, then quits (40%+ annual turnover in commercial PM). A property manager tracks lease renewals in a spreadsheet, misses a 60-day notice window, and loses leverage on a rent escalation. Yardi logs everything after the fact but doesn't pick up the phone, send the chase email, or verify the policy.

Every piece of the operation is handled by a different person, vendor, or workaround. And every time someone leaves, the whole thing degrades.

Nobody owns the full operations layer for commercial real estate. Until now.

## 3. What Premise AI Actually Is

Premise AI takes over three operational functions that commercial landlords currently spread across BPO vendors, internal staff, and manual processes:

### Function 1: Tenant & Vendor Communication
Every inbound and outbound interaction - maintenance requests, HVAC, vendor access, inquiries, follow-ups - handled across chat, SMS, and email. AI resolves 70-90% automatically. A human ops desk handles the rest.

### Function 2: Compliance Collection
COIs chased, collected, extracted, and verified against lease requirements. Certified gross sales data collected from retail tenants for percentage rent. Vendor credentialing managed end-to-end. The full process, not just tracking.

### Function 3: Lease Event Management
AI reads abstracted lease data and triggers outbound communications at every milestone - rent escalations, renewal windows, CAM estimates, percentage rent true-ups. Nothing gets missed. Nothing is late.

**The model:**
The customer pays a monthly fee. They get the AI platform, a staffed human ops desk, knowledge base setup, ongoing QA, SLA reporting, and 2-week onboarding. They do nothing operationally after go-live.

## 4. Positioning Pillars

### Pillar 1: "We run it. You don't."
Every pure-SaaS competitor (EliseAI, Funnel, AppFolio, RealPage) requires the customer to hire or redirect staff to run escalations, manage QA, train agents, and maintain the knowledge base. Traditional BPOs answer calls but have no commercial lease knowledge. Premise AI eliminates both problems.

**Proof point:** Staff turnover in commercial PM exceeds 40% annually. Every new hire degrades a SaaS implementation. With Premise, the customer's staff turnover is irrelevant because Premise runs the system.

### Pillar 2: "Built for commercial complexity."
Percentage rent clauses. Tenant COI verification against lease minimums. Rent escalation notices. Renewal option windows. CAM reconciliation delivery. Vendor credentialing. Estoppel collection during transactions. These workflows don't exist in multifamily. Multifamily tools can't handle them.

**Proof point:** QuadReal went from 500 compliance checks/year to 3,500/year without adding staff. That's 7x output on the same headcount.

### Pillar 3: "SLA-backed outcomes, not feature lists."
Every SLA is timestamped, measured, and reported monthly. If commitments aren't met, the fee is waived.

| Commitment | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Contact response - business hours | < 2 hours |
| Contact response - after hours | < 4 hours |
| Tenant COI compliance rate | 95%+ |
| Retail sales data collection rate | 90%+ |
| Lease event on-time rate | 100% |

**Proof point:** In a discovery call, ask any prospect: "How many of your tenants have a current, verified COI on file right now?" They can't answer. Premise can show them a dashboard.

### Pillar 4: "Your tenants know their breakpoints better than you do."
(Retail ICP-specific) Percentage rent revenue leakage through tenant underreporting is an unsolved, high-pain problem. Premise collects, verifies, and certifies gross sales data - catching underreporting and exclusion claims that would otherwise go unchecked.

**Proof point:** Certified, audit-defensible sales data with CPA review. Not a tracking tool - a managed collection and verification process.
